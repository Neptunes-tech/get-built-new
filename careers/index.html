What We Value

Our team is built on 11 Guiding Principles that not only set us apart, but form the foundation for all that we do. These core values are how we can ensure that as we build our team, we’re building for success.
Join Our Crew

Be a Team Player

Our best success comes when our team is passionate about the work they do and the results they deliver. We’re a passionate team in love with the problem, not the solution. We celebrate victories, learn from our mistakes, and strive for humility in everything.

Put People First

We serve our team and clients by putting them first. This means setting people up for success, being thoughtful in our communication, and working hard to build honest relationships.

Work with Passion

We believe the best teams are made up of hard workers who solve the right problems at the right time. Through collaboration, we follow through on our commitments, have each other’s backs, and have a good time doing it.

Every Builter Belongs

At Built we are all about building— from houses and skyscrapers to careers and relationships. Our Diversity & Inclusion committee’s mission is to champion every Builter as they build extraordinary and fulfilling careers on a foundation of trust, collaboration, innovation, and leadership by dismantling discriminatory barriers, facilitating meaningful conversations and education, and celebrating our diversity.

D&I in Action

Promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion starts with having strategic initiatives in place to prioritize this within Built and the way that we operate in our community. Our D&I Committee has special focus areas such as Strategic Initiatives, Education, Hiring, Community, Employee Resource Groups (ERGs), and D&I Book Club.

Committed to Growth

Our company is made up of passionate people who are driven in a variety of disciplines—and each of them brings their unique perspective to everything they do. Creating a safe and inclusive workplace is critical to the success of our company. As we learn and as we grow, we’re committed to ensuring these ideals are at the forefront of everything we do.

Benefits for Builters

We’re dedicated to your success—in and outside of the office. Prioritizing the health and wellness of our employees is a top priority. Here are a few of the benefits we offer every Builter:

  • Time Off

    Uncapped paid time off in addition to 6 company holidays and 6 ‘lite’ holidays

  • Healthcare

    Comprehensive healthcare package offerings to choose from for you and your family

  • Compensation

    Total compensation package including a bonus and equity program

  • Learning

    Learning grants to support your growth & development

  • Flexibility

    Flexible working hours and hybrid work options to ensure you’re getting your best work done, your way

  • Community

    Opportunities to make an impact beyond your role including D&I, ERGs, Sustainability, and Community Service committees
